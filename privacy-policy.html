Privacy Policy

Cybesis Screensavers

Cybesis Studios • Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Introduction

Cybesis Studios ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Cybesis Screensavers collection of applications (each, the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This App is designed to operate with minimal data collection.

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
  • Location Data: We do not collect or track your location
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the App
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers or system specifications
  • Browsing History: We do not access or collect browsing history
  • Files or Photos: We do not access your files (except background images you explicitly select)

Information We MAY Collect (Pro Version Only)

Pro upgrades are one-time in-app purchases processed by the Microsoft Store, and Microsoft's privacy terms apply. License status is verified through Microsoft's licensing APIs. We do not receive or store your Microsoft account information.

Data Storage

All configuration and license data is stored locally on your device only. No data is stored on our servers.

  • Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%\<ScreensaverName>\config.json (one folder per screensaver, e.g. DigitalRainSaver)

Third-Party Services

Microsoft Store: Microsoft processes payments (including in-app Pro purchases) and manages licenses. See Microsoft Privacy Statement.

Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell, share, or use your data for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all data stored by the App (check configuration files)
  • Delete: Remove all App data by uninstalling or deleting configuration files
  • Portability: Export your configuration by copying the config file

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is rated for all ages (PEGI 3 / ESRB Everyone).

Security

Pro licenses are verified through the Microsoft Store licensing system. No sensitive data is transmitted in plain text.

TL;DR: We don't collect your data. The App works offline. Pro upgrades are in-app purchases processed by the Microsoft Store, and license verification requires only minimal data exchange with Microsoft. Your settings are stored locally on your device.
