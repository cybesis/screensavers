# Digital Rain Matrix Screensaver

> **Status: Available now on the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9ND4MDXV97GS)**

Transform your idle screen into the iconic Matrix digital rain effect. Watch cascading green glyphs flow down your display with smooth, movie-accurate animation — mirrored katakana, a flickering bright head, and authentic phosphor-green color, running at a silky 60 FPS.

**Developed by [Cybesis Studios](https://cybesis.com) — an indie solo developer**

---

## ✨ Features

### The Authentic Matrix Look
- **Movie-Accurate Glyphs**: Horizontally mirrored katakana, just like the real Matrix code font
- **Phosphor-Green Palette**: The signature deep `#00FF41` green, not a flat lime
- **Head Flicker**: The bright leading glyph rapidly scrambles as it falls
- **Smooth Fall-Off**: Streams slide cleanly off the bottom edge instead of popping out

### Visual Customization
- **9 Character Sets**: Katakana (classic Matrix), Binary, Digits, Symbols, Hexadecimal, ASCII, Cyrillic, Greek, and Runic — plus your own **Custom characters**
- **6 Color Themes**: Green, Blue (Cyan), Red, White, Amber, and Purple
- **Rain Direction**: Down, Up, Left, or Right — watch the Matrix flow any way you like
- **Speed, Density, Brightness & Zoom**: Fine-tune the motion and feel of the rain
- **Frame Rate**: Choose 15, 24, 30, or 60 FPS to balance smoothness and efficiency

### Cinematic Effects
- **Glyph Flash**: Random glyphs flash bright white — the iconic Matrix shimmer
- **Head Glow Bloom**: A soft glow around the leading glyph of each stream
- **Color Intensity**: Dial the saturation of the rain up or down

### Interactive Elements
- **Mouse Ripple Effects**: Optional ripple distortion that follows your cursor
- **Custom Messages**: Display your own text over the rain
- **Custom Background Image**: Blend your own image behind the rain
- **Digital Clock**: Built-in clock overlay with auto or custom color

### Performance & Compatibility
- **Smooth 60 FPS**: Optimized Canvas rendering via Microsoft Edge WebView2
- **Multi-Monitor Support**: Automatically scales complexity across multiple displays
- **Lightweight**: Minimal memory footprint, runs efficiently on modern PCs
- **Offline Capable**: No internet connection required

## 📥 Installation

### From the Microsoft Store
Get the latest version with automatic updates from the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9ND4MDXV97GS).

After installing:
1. Open **Digital Rain** from the Start Menu — it sets itself up as your active screensaver and opens the settings window.
2. Customize it however you like, then click **Save Settings**.
3. Use the **Preview** button to see it full-screen, or just let it kick in when your PC goes idle.

You can adjust your idle wait time any time in Windows **Screen Saver Settings** (search "screen saver" in the Start menu).

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Screensaver doesn't start on its own**
- Set an idle wait time in Windows **Screen Saver Settings**.
- Make sure another app (media player, presentation mode) isn't keeping the system awake.
- Open the Digital Rain app once from the Start Menu so it registers itself as the active screensaver.

**It's not in the Windows screensaver dropdown**
- Store apps install per-user, so the classic dropdown may not list it — that's expected. The app sets itself as your active screensaver when you launch it, and the in-app **Preview** button confirms it's working.

**Settings don't save / reset**
- Settings live in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\DigitalRainSaver\config.json`.
- If they won't stick, your antivirus or a managed-device policy may be blocking the app's setup step — temporarily allow the app and re-launch it from the Start Menu.

**Performance issues / low FPS**
- Lower the Frame Rate to 30 FPS, reduce Density, or try the **Eco Mode** preset.
- Turn off Head Glow Bloom.
- Update your graphics drivers.

**Black screen instead of animation**
- Install the [Microsoft Edge WebView2 Runtime](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkId=2124703) (usually pre-installed on Windows 10/11).
