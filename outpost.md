# Outpost Tower Screensaver

> **Status: Available now on the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9NJ1DQHFWH6H)**

Turn your idle screen into a quiet watch post — a looping window onto the field with ambience, a live radar feed with animated contacts, and a rotating hall of medals. Calm, cinematic, and just a little tactical.

**Developed by [Cybesis Studios](https://cybesis.com) — an indie solo developer**

---

## ✨ Features

### Three Cinematic Display Modes

#### Window of Outpost
A looping video window onto the field with background ambience — like gazing out of a quiet watch post. Control ambience volume, mute, and cover/fit. Pro users can bring their own looping outpost video.

#### Radar
A live radar sweep with animated contacts and data panels — a calm, cinematic tactical feed.

#### Medal Showcase
Three rotating medals with glow effects — a slowly turning hall of honors.

### Visual Customization
- **7 Color Themes**: Including Steel Honor, Bronze Victory, and Naval Command
- **8 Cinematic Video Filters** (Pro): Grayscale, sepia, warm, cool, night, and more
- **Custom Motto Overlay** (Pro): Your own text as a cinematic overlay
- **Ambience Controls**: Volume, mute, and cover/fit for the video window

### Performance & Reliability
- **Burn-In Protection**: Guards OLED and AMOLED panels
- **Multi-Monitor Support** (Pro): The outpost on every connected display
- **Smooth Rendering**: Optimized via Microsoft Edge WebView2

## 📥 Installation

### From the Microsoft Store
Get the latest version with automatic updates from the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9NJ1DQHFWH6H).

After installing:
1. Open **Outpost Tower** from the Start Menu — it registers itself as your active screensaver and opens settings.
2. Choose your mode and theme, then click **Save Settings**.
3. Set your idle wait time in Windows **Screen Saver Settings**, or click **Preview** to test immediately.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Screensaver doesn't start automatically**
- Set an idle wait time in Windows **Screen Saver Settings** (search "screen saver" in the Start menu).
- Open the Outpost Tower app from the Start Menu once to register it.

**Settings don't save**
- Settings are stored in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\OutpostSaver\config.json`.
- If they won't persist, your antivirus may be blocking the app — allow it and re-launch.

**Black screen instead of animation**
- Install the [Microsoft Edge WebView2 Runtime](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkId=2124703) (usually pre-installed on Windows 10/11).
