# Living Flags Screensaver

> **Status: Available now on the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9N7MGKLD0RHK)**

A celebration of the world's nations — a waving national flag in real cloth, a living mosaic of flags, all lit like a warm sunset. Pick any of 250+ countries, free.

**Developed by [Cybesis Studios](https://cybesis.com) — an indie solo developer**

---

## ✨ Features

### Two Cinematic Display Modes

#### Honor Flag
A waving cloth flag with fabric textures under a sunset sky. Choose any nation — 250+ country flags are included, free.

#### Mosaic
A living mosaic of the world's flags with multiple layouts:
- **Random Grid**: A shifting patchwork of flags from around the world
- **Single-Flag Tile**: Fill the mosaic with your chosen nation
- **Kaleidoscope**: Symmetric, ever-turning flag patterns
- **Wave / Morph**: Tiles ripple and morph across the screen

### Visual Customization
- **250+ Country Flags**: Pick any nation — free, no upgrade needed
- **8 Warm Color Themes**: Sunset Glory, Dawn Calm, Old Glory, Tricolore, Emerald Isle, Royal Azure, Golden Hour, Twilight
- **Sunset Lighting**: Every scene is lit like a warm sunset
- **Custom Motto Overlay** (Pro): Your own text as a cinematic overlay
- **Atmospheric Fog** (Pro): Depth haze for added drama

### Performance & Reliability
- **Burn-In Protection**: Guards OLED and AMOLED panels
- **Multi-Monitor Support** (Pro): Flags on every connected display
- **Fully Offline**: No internet connection required
- **Smooth Rendering**: Optimized via Microsoft Edge WebView2

## 📥 Installation

### From the Microsoft Store
Get the latest version with automatic updates from the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9N7MGKLD0RHK).

After installing:
1. Open **Living Flags** from the Start Menu — it registers itself as your active screensaver and opens settings.
2. Choose your mode, nation, and theme, then click **Save Settings**.
3. Set your idle wait time in Windows **Screen Saver Settings**, or click **Preview** to test immediately.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Screensaver doesn't start automatically**
- Set an idle wait time in Windows **Screen Saver Settings** (search "screen saver" in the Start menu).
- Open the Living Flags app from the Start Menu once to register it.

**Settings don't save**
- Settings are stored in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\LivingFlagsSaver\config.json`.
- If they won't persist, your antivirus may be blocking the app — allow it and re-launch.

**Black screen instead of animation**
- Install the [Microsoft Edge WebView2 Runtime](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkId=2124703) (usually pre-installed on Windows 10/11).
